Benefits of Playing Badminton & Coaching

Playing badminton in Indoor Court is the easiest way to lose weight. Staying fit and in shape require regular physical activity, playing badminton helps you to do that. It helps you to lose minimum 2 – 4 kg in your first month of playing. You burn more than 400 calories in just an hour without struggling. No other physical activity can help you to lose weight with as much as fun you get in the shuttle game. Playing badminton for at least 30 – 40 minutes every day can be your perfect fitness regime. With badminton getting you in great shape is easy and fun.
Playing Badminton is not just for fun, it will give incredible changes in kids and adults. It will improve social skills, like making friends, being good at the teamwork. It enhances the physical attributes of the body which are most necessary for muscular strength and endurance. It helps to lower the bad cholesterol. Bad cholesterol reduces the size of the blood vessel that can trigger a heart attack and stroke.

Little Flyers or Beginners Badminton Training

Below are the reasons why your child should attend our camp.
1. Physically active: As Children sit down at home ideally for the entire day inside the house; our camp gives your child so much time to spend to move, run, jog and play at our badminton court.
2. Unplug from Smart Gadgets: When the child gets disconnected from television and smartphones, they discover the creativity and gets connected to the outside world and improve socializing skills.
3. Confidence and Success: Our non-competitive activities in our camp will help your child to build self-esteem, confidence.
4. Grow Independent: Our camp is the best place to practice and make decisions independently without the interference of parents and teachers.
5. Social Skills: In our Camp, everyone has to agree to co-operate, share and respect each other. This will improve their socializing skills.
